SEASONS ABOUT HOBBIES

Season 1 Discussion

The Club is (Almost) Dead

Yui Hirasawa joins high school and wants to be in a club… but she’s clueless and picks the Light Music Club thinking it’s “something light and easy.” mio, ritsu and mugi rope Yui in, despite the fact that she can’t play a single instrument.

Yui gets her first Guitar

The girls convince Yui to learn guitar.she goes through adorable struggles like her fingers hurting, not understanding chords, and needing lessons.

Mio Fan Club Incident

Mio accidentally becomes the “cool, mysterious beauty” of the school after their performance.She gains an entire fan club .Much to her embarrassment, she becomes the idol of the Light Music Club, and it leads to a lot of teasing (especially from Ritsu).

First Performance

The girls start getting serious when school events come up.Mio writes lyrics (often embarrassed), Yui struggles but improves, and they eventually perform as a group.Their first big live: the school festival, where Yui (after losing her voice from a cold) sings and plays with all her heart.

Season 2 Discussion

The Club's Last Year Together

Yui, Mio, Ritsu, and Mugi are now third-years.Azusa is the only second-year, meaning she’ll be left alone when they graduate.This season really leans into the theme of “enjoying every moment before graduation.

Events

School: FestivalAnother big performance, showing how much they’ve improved.

Feild Trip:Yui, Mio, Ritsu, and Mugi travel and make memories together.

Summer Training Camp:Azusa tries to make them practice seriously… but they end up playing around (classic HTT).

Mio's Fan Club Returns: Still hilariously embarrassing for her.

New Year and exams: They juggle band life with preparing for college entrance exams.

Graduation

The senior pass their exams and prepare to leave high school. They perform one last time together at the school festival emotional and bittersweet.A lot of season 2 is about azusa's feelings she loves her senpai but dreads being left behind. Azusa cries,but the girls sing special farewell song for thanking her for being part of their lives.
